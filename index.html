Meme Generator

This app is a meme generator.

Here's how to use it : select an image from the gallery or upload one from your computer, and edit it in the meme editor.

There, you can insert multiple lines and edit each line of text.

Then, you can save it to your memes, and go back to editing it later from the memes gallery.

You can also choose to download the meme to your computer, or share it on FaceBook.

Have Fun !
